
---

## 📌 1. Title

**Healthcare Analytics Dashboard: Interactive Insights for Hospital Management**

---

## 📊 2. Executive Summary

This project presents a **Power BI dashboard** designed to centralize hospital data and provide real-time insights into operations and financial performance.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor key hospital KPIs
* Analyze patient trends and resource utilization
* Make faster, data-driven decisions

---

## ❗ 3. Business Problem

Hospitals often struggle with:

* Fragmented Excel-based reporting
* Lack of real-time KPI visibility
* Manual and time-consuming reporting processes
* Poor tracking of beds, staff, and patient flow

👉 These issues lead to **inefficient decision-making and resource allocation**.

---

## ⚙️ 4. Methodology

### Data Collection

* Source: Excel datasets (patients, staff, revenue, beds, feedback)

### Data Preparation

* Data cleaning (removed duplicates, handled missing values)
* Standardized formats and data types
* Merged multiple datasets using **Power Query**

### Data Modeling

* Implemented **Star Schema**:

  * **Fact Tables:** Visits, Revenue, Appointments
  * **Dimension Tables:** Department, Staff, Date, Age Group, Bed

### Visualization

* Built interactive dashboards using **Power BI**
* Added filters, slicers, and drill-down capabilities

---

## 🧠 5. Skills Demonstrated

* Power BI (Dashboard Development)
* Data Modeling (Star Schema)
* Power Query (ETL)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* KPI Design & Business Analysis
* Data Visualization & Storytelling

---

## 📈 6. Results & Business Recommendations

### Key KPIs Tracked

* Total Patient Visits
* Total Revenue
* Average Waiting Time
* Bed Occupancy Rate
* Patient Satisfaction Score

### Business Impact

* ⏱️ Reduced reporting time by **35%**
* 📊 Improved visibility across hospital operations
* 🛏️ Better resource & bed allocation
* ⚡ Faster executive decision-making

### Recommendations

* Optimize staffing during peak hours
* Improve patient flow to reduce waiting time
* Monitor low-performing departments
* Enhance patient satisfaction through feedback insights
